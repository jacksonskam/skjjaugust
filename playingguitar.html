2025/02/13 - The Death of Queen Jane. This one is an old English folk song that has been widely covered but this arrangement is most akin to Oscar Isaac's from the movie Inside Llewyn Davis. I really love the story of this song and its historical references to King Henry VIII and one of his wives Jane Seymour who was the only of Henry's wives to receive a burial after she died from postnatal complications two weeks after giving birth to the future King Edward VI. The song's female perspective is a great flip from the usual male-dominated retelling of history.
